How to play:

The computer thinks about a word.
In the box you can write letters and the computer will check if your letter is in the word.
If your letter is in the word the computer will change the underscore to the letter you wrote.
If your letter isn't in the word the computer will draw a part of the man.
To win you need to discover the word before the man is hanged.
If the man is hanged you lose the game.
Good luck!

To move to the game click on the picture.
